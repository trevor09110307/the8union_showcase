THE 8-UNION SHOWCASE

SINCE 2018, THE 8-UNION SHOWCASE HAS BROUGHT LOCAL KIDS, STREETBALL LEGENDS AND PROFESSIONAL STARS FROM AROUND USA TOGETHER IN BOSTON — ALL FOR THE LOVE OF BASKETBALL. THE RICH LEGACY INCLUDES THE ORIGINAL FOUNDING FATHER, A VISIONARY COMMISSIONER, VETERAN COACHES, THREE DIFFERENT GYMS AND COUNTLESS MAGICAL MOMENTS. THE 8-UNION SHOWCASE HAS BEEN A REMARKABLE SUCCESS STORY THAT BEGAN IN SOUTH CENTRAL AND STILL CONTINUES TO FLOURISH WITHIN THE EXACT SAME COMMUNITY.
